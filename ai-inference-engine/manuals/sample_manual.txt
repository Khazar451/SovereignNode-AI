=============================================================================
SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW
=============================================================================

The AquaDrive XP-7000 is a heavy-duty centrifugal pump designed for continuous
industrial fluid transfer applications. Operating range: 5 to 450 m3/h, head
up to 120 m, maximum fluid temperature 150 degrees Celsius.

Drive configuration: Direct-drive, coupled to a 75 kW three-phase induction
motor rated 415V / 50 Hz. Shaft seal: Double mechanical seal, pressurized
barrier fluid system (API Plan 53B).

=============================================================================
SECTION 2 — ROUTINE MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE
=============================================================================

2.1 Daily Checks (Operator)
---------------------------
- Verify bearing temperature does not exceed 80 degrees Celsius (threshold alert at 75 C).
- Check mechanical seal barrier fluid pressure: nominal 2.5 bar, alarm at 2.0 bar.
- Inspect for abnormal noise or vibration. Baseline vibration: < 2.8 mm/s RMS.
- Confirm lubricant level in bearing housing (sight glass: between MIN and MAX marks).
- Log pump flow rate and differential pressure against design curve.

2.2 Weekly Checks (Maintenance Technician)
------------------------------------------
- Grease both bearing housings: SKF LGMT 3 grease, 5 ml per housing via grease nipple.
- Inspect flexible coupling for wear, misalignment, or elastomer degradation.
- Verify motor current draw against nameplate FLA (Full Load Amps = 128 A at 415V).
- Test emergency stop circuit and interlock function.

2.3 Monthly Checks
-------------------
- Replace mechanical seal barrier fluid: use Shell Tellus S2 VX 32 hydraulic oil.
- Full vibration analysis (ISO 10816-3 Class III): replace bearings if vibration exceeds 7.1 mm/s RMS.
- Inspect impeller for cavitation erosion; replace if blade thickness < 3 mm.
- Retorque pump casing bolts to 180 Nm (M20 grade 8.8 bolts).

2.4 Annual Overhaul
--------------------
- Complete disassembly, inspection, and reassembly by certified pump technician.
- Replace mechanical seal cartridge (Part No: XP7-SEAL-053B).
- Replace all O-rings and gaskets (Kit Part No: XP7-GASK-KIT).
- Rebalance impeller to ISO 1940 Grade G2.5.
- Perform hydraulic performance test against original factory curve.

=============================================================================
SECTION 3 — TROUBLESHOOTING
=============================================================================

3.1 Excessive Vibration (> 4.5 mm/s RMS)
-----------------------------------------
Possible causes:
  a) Cavitation — check NPSHa vs NPSHr; verify suction pressure.
  b) Impeller imbalance — inspect and rebalance.
  c) Bearing wear — check radial play; replace if clearance > 0.05 mm.
  d) Shaft misalignment — realign using laser alignment tool (tolerance: 0.05 mm/m).
  e) Foundation looseness — re-grout baseplate.

3.2 Mechanical Seal Leakage
----------------------------
Acceptable leakage: < 3 drops/minute for single mechanical seals.
If leakage exceeds 3 drops/minute:
  a) Check barrier fluid pressure (Plan 53B): restore to 2.5 bar.
  b) Inspect seal faces for scoring or thermal cracking.
  c) Replace seal cartridge (Part No: XP7-SEAL-053B). Torque gland bolts to 45 Nm.

3.3 High Bearing Temperature (> 80 C)
---------------------------------------
  a) Check lubricant level and quality — discolouration indicates contamination.
  b) Verify grease type: only SKF LGMT 3 approved.
  c) Check for overloading: measure shaft power vs nameplate rating.
  d) Replace bearing if temperature persists after re-lubrication (FAG 6316-2RSR-C3).

3.4 Low Flow / Low Pressure
-----------------------------
  a) Check for partially closed suction or discharge valve.
  b) Inspect impeller — worn or blocked passages reduce hydraulic performance.
  c) Check for air entrainment in suction line.
  d) Verify speed (VFD output frequency): rated 50 Hz. Adjust if below setpoint.

=============================================================================
SECTION 5 — SAFETY REQUIREMENTS
=============================================================================

- De-energize and LOTO (Lock-Out Tag-Out) before any maintenance.
- Drain and flush the pump casing before opening — hazardous fluid risk.
- Use PPE: chemical-resistant gloves, face shield, steel-capped boots.
- Hot surfaces: pump casing may reach 150 C under normal operation.
- Minimum two-person team required for impeller removal (weight: 48 kg).
- Emergency shutdown: press red E-STOP button on MCC panel or use DCS tag PMP-7000-ESD.
